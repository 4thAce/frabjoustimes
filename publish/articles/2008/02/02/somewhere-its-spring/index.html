Somewhere it's Spring

Sat, Feb 2, 2008

a lark's song
and she wakes -- it's just a dream
of Earth


through leafmould sapphires burn: Seventh Life


early dawn --
windshields clear of
ammonia frost


next up to bat... Homo habilis


this place of red,
turning... the first of
many worms


when sulphur flows
down living rock,
remember me
